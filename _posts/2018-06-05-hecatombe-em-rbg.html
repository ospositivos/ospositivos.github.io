Por cá a mrnrg-cenas fecha portas e anuncia-o online - parecido, supomos, com outras notícias em digital no reino das edições alternativas, apenas ao contrário: Hecatombe editions, a small Swiss publishing house de longo historial em fanzines embarcou nos digital comics com uma coleção dedicada de nome "RGB Collection". Inchem, CMYKs.

Very clearly on the limits of the experimental side of comics - an intransigent successor to the underground alternative edition of the last decades of the 20th century, a mix between the webcomic procedural and participatory creation.
in "La tournée numérique de Phylacterium : mai 2018" maio 2018

Nota: as citações que se seguem foram traduzidas do frances por autobots e retalhadas para brevidade por urs truly. Inchem, humanos.

A political vision of publishing

De proximidades,

Hecatombe has always defended a rather radical place in the alternative movement, defending the principles of self-publishing, lack of profit, control by the author of all stages of the book's making.
in "La tournée numérique de Phylacterium : mai 2018" maio 2018

- e diferenças: i) uma visão política mas míope por via dos suspeitos do costume, artsys:

Often flirting with abstraction - a catalog certainly a little hermetic to the uninitiated.
in "La tournée numérique de Phylacterium : mai 2018" maio 2018

- e ii) aquilo do $$$ que parece nunca andar muito longe em intenções:

The ambition of the RGB Collection is thus to offer authors a distribution structure for digital works (and digital) paying.
in "La tournée numérique de Phylacterium : mai 2018" maio 2018

Não querendo terminar apresentações nessa nota, onde importa:

To sum it up, the RGB Collection is a digital edition that applies the ethics of alternative publishing.
in "La tournée numérique de Phylacterium : mai 2018" maio 2018

Da coleção:

Hécatombe defends the idea that digital must not eliminate the social interactions of comics, hence a "physical" version that can be distributed in bookstores or on loan. Too early to say if it's a movement of substance, but the reconciliation between the alternative print edition and the digital creation is emerging little by little and pose the question of an alternative digital edition.
in "La tournée numérique de Phylacterium : mai 2018" maio 2018

E onde nos toca:

Web-based creation always had important similarities with alternative principles and fanzines in that it plays a fairly similar role as a creative laboratory for the entire profession: ideal self-publishing, not profit-seeking, direct contact with readers, exacerbated creative freedom, a taste for experimentation and trial-and-error.
in "La tournée numérique de Phylacterium : mai 2018" maio 2018

Com tanta liberdade e tão poucas penalizações em contrário, é de estranhar o estado da arte em cómicos.

Until now, the movement went in the other direction, from digital to print.
in "La tournée numérique de Phylacterium : mai 2018" maio 2018
