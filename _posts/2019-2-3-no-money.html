[Q] I see as one of your central artistic concerns here [is] capitalism and the idea of bosses.
[Mickey Z] It was on my mind a lot. I thought a lot about capitalism as being a massive trap that everybody is just stuck in, because everyone has to make money to survive so, you have to participate somehow [and] any participation just keeps the wheels turning.
in "'Uhhh I Haven't Heard This, About Me Being an Artistic Hero Haha': A Conversation with Mickey Zacchilli" 18 jan 2019

Da direcção que essas wheels vão virar num futuro próximo, para um futuro mais próximo ainda, esta entrada é preliminar desse. Em cómicos e $$$ o nosso interesse por BD comercial não é inocente: importa-nos pela mesma razão que nos importam os media: ferramenta bem sucedida de comunicação às massas. Mas sendo os P+ um zine em sentido inverso — massas, e/ou sucesso — começamos por espreitar aquela primeira pequena peça a espremer nessa indústria: o humano chamado autor. Para iniciar hostilidades destaque da última newsletter do The Beat: "it's time to get paid".

This week's twitter convo was kicked off when Declan Shalvey tweeted: 'there’s certain newer publishers out there who have not been paying their creators. NOTICE: creators are openly talking about this amongst themselves for months. Unpaid creators eventually have nothing to lose, so for everyone’s sake, PAY THEM.' I had a pretty good idea who he was referring to, but after the DMs starting rolling I heard UP TO 7 PUBLISHERS that they could be talking about; a few on the list that surprised me. Some of the companies that are falling behind mean well, but...comics publishing isn't all that lucrative. We told you that when you arrived for the party. The publishers who are struggling to make ends meet mostly publish new up-and-coming creators. And that includes some of the most acclaimed new books of 2018, I'm sad to say. The odds were always against these books making mone.
in The (Comics) Beat newsletter 24 jan 2019

E não se pense que é um problema exclusivo a certos novos publishers. Mesma altura, outra peça:

Multiple comics sites published articles in the last couple of days regarding DC Comics, which has begun the process of firing people in an attempt to restructure the company into one that appears more profitable via the easiest method possible.
in "Seattle Bon" 25 jan 2019

Noutro planeta outros autores experienciam um bliss de fun apenas possível quando alcançam aquele nirvana que desliga comics e capital.

It's unfortunately extremely universal haha.

Fun is what I pursue when I make comics, absolutely. If I'm not having fun drawing comics, why am I drawing comics? I used to think I wanted to be a career cartoonist, or something, but everything connected to that made it not very fun, and stressful, and I'd make work just because I felt like I had to, I'd make work for all the wrong reasons, or when I didn't feel like making work, which made everything very not fun and it didn't make me happy. I think this ties into the whole capitalism thing we talked about before.
in "'Uhhh I Haven't Heard This, About Me Being an Artistic Hero Haha': A Conversation with Mickey Zacchilli" 18 jan 2019
