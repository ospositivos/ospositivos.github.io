A marcar pontos pela indieweb nesta semana, o nosso resumo do resumo da "The History of the Web" e o uso popular nesta do termo mainstream media 10 dez 2024. Reportando-se ao Chomsky e o seu "What Makes Mainstream Media Mainstream" out 1997, quando a "World Wide Web hadn’t quite yet brought publishing to the masses":

Of course, the serious stuff is for the big guys. “We” take care of that.

A power structure designed to funnel people through elite institutions and universities and into centers of power and influence [...] filters out individuals who stray from that worldview. [...] The result, Chomsky argues, creates a homogeneity of perspective within the news, “real news,” also known as the elite media, or agenda-setting news.

Obviamente regressamos à feliz intersecção de tech e informação dos 90's

These bloggers set themselves apart from their “serious” predecessors as often as they could.

The late 1990’s coincided with a rise of indie blogging. Publishing on the web, where costs were near zero, meant that anyone could do it. And lots of people who may have been shut out from traditional media created their own identities online. Bloggers could use a personal and distinct voice and tone, blog at whatever frequency they wished, and write about their own areas of interest. It was decidedly different than its counterpart in traditional media. They imagined blogging as something more authentic, on the ground, unencumbered by the pressures of the elite-driven media industry.
in "Where did mainstream media come from?" 10 dez 2024

Obvia e mente, a reação dos profissionais da informação foi a que preencheu páginas e páginas de difamação aos bárbaros no portão: "many treated blogging as unserious, a hobby of amateurs, not to be trusted". Carinhos devolvidos, poucos blogs não têm no seu ethos um desmascarar da "authenticity and trustworthiness" dos media. Tentou-se inicialmente mesma receita contra as redes sociais mas esse comboio partiu da estação quando chegados à Era do Telemóvel para nunca mais voltar. Ao contrário dos indieblogs, formato longo, longa memória, com os quais a convivência foi terrivelmente inconveniente, nenhuma infâmia consegue afastar estes mainstream media dos social media que merecem. Porque coabitam tão harmoniosamente com este formato massivo, efémero, estridente, mais das vezes manchados de desinformação e outros propósitos nefastos, é-nos um mistério.

Most have no idea they are a willing participant. [...] Try as they might, it is near impossible for those who exist within mainstream media to remove themselves from it.
