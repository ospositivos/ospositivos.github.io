In 2021 a user described the “dead internet theory: most of the internet is fake,” which claimed that since 2016, the majority of online activity has been driven by bots operated by shadowy actors with a desire to shape public opinion. The Dead Internet Theory stands out insofar as [...] acknowledging the paradigm shift we’ve seen on the Web over the past decade.

The rise of AI has effectively broken with core web functionality in a drive to maximise user engagement. The idea of an intuitive web — one where you instinctively know what things do, and can confidently predict the results of each action — died when tech companies shoved AI into features where it has no right to exist.
