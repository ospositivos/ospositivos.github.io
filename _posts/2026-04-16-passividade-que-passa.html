Só temos coisas boas a dizer do RSS dos outros. Alguns highlights,

Anti-Algorithm/Anti-Doomscrolling: It allows for a calm, intentional consumption of content, freeing users from the addictive nature of social media feeds.

Resilience & Longevity: As an open protocol, RSS is not subject to the whims of a single company or platform shutdowns, making it a sustainable tool for long-term blogging.

Ownership & Autonomy: RSS allows creators to own their content and audience, keeping control over distribution, unlike social media where platforms control, suppress, or gatekeep content.
