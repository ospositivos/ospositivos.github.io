Seguimos de revoluções e IA, desta jornalismo.

I am an optimist. I believe that we are in a new golden age of journalism. And that the explosion of new platforms has opened up new routes for journalists to reach consumers with more original, thoughtful, intelligent writing and storytelling than ever before. In a world of AI slop and exploitative algorithms, consumers are seeking out this journalism, and choosing human-to-human connections.
in "A warning to the news industry: act now or the Joe Rogan/Piers Morgan ecosystem will leave you far behind" 12 maio 2026

Somos positivos por (de?) graça, e acudidos de uma imensa falta de fé na humanidade: nós *não* acreditamos. Do texto citado o que podem depositar no banco, consensual entre casta, retalhado, porque se há aqui alguma novidade não nos andam a ler de tempos imemoriais:

there is a revolution reshaping how people want and get their information

I believe that the established media hasn’t confronted the hard truth – that this revolution isn’t just about consumers moving to different platforms. It’s that they are choosing more direct forms of journalism. This creator journalism is not a sideshow. It is fast becoming the show.
in "A warning to the news industry: act now or the Joe Rogan/Piers Morgan ecosystem will leave you far behind" 12 maio 2026

Um "genuinely digital-first model" ("to produce and distribute content in the formats and on the platforms that consumers want") e aquilo do habitual, se de tempos imemoriais nos leem:

I believe this will require established media players to face up to the consequences of the shift of trust and attention from institutions to individuals; from programmes and platforms to people. And to respond to the consumer desire for authentic, independent voices.
in "A warning to the news industry: act now or the Joe Rogan/Piers Morgan ecosystem will leave you far behind" 12 maio 2026

Daqui ao declínio das instituições noticiosas apenas um pequeno parágrafo, tecnológico mas civilizacional: a mudança em curso é estrutural e comportamental. Onde concordamos: "the move away from mass reach, and its replacement with a fragmented media landscape, is what defines this revolution." Onde estão a ser ingénuos porque humanos serão humanos: se a escolha recai entre "authentic, independent" ou "more direct forms of journalism", a IA come esse almoço.
