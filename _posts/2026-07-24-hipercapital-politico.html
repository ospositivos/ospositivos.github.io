do recém-falecido Pat Oliphant, the quiet man with the loud drawings, cartoon dos anos 2000, quando os norte-américas votaram num presidente e elegeram outro

Trazemos-vos Anton Jäger’s "Hyperpolitics: Extreme Politicization without Political Consequences", cujas referências filosóficas são Deleuze & Guattari, Mouffe & Laclau (já resumidos no nosso espaço - ide ler...), e, pelo próprio, "simplesmente marxista". Resumimos resumo:

His thinking runs as follows. The years 1914 to 1989 mark the age of mass politics, when politicisation was high and institutionalisation was also high. Then, 1989 to the financial crash in 2008 was the age of post-politics, when institutionalisation was low and politicisation was through the floor. From the crash through to about 2020 was the age of anti-politics, politicisation came back with a vengeance with institutional ambitions. Hyperpolitics is what came next: intense politicisation, with neither institutional roots nor ambitions.
